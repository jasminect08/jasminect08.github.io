About Me

Hi There

I am a graphic designer based in the Maryland and Washington D.C. Metro Area with 5+ years of creative experience. First and foremost, I love black, a good cup of coffee, and you have my heart if you bring me donuts (Krispy Kreme does not count). My work has varied from editorial design, to animation, to web design. But what has remained consistent, is my core belief: As a designer, my responsibility is to solve my clients problems. There’s no reason you can’t have both function and beauty. But it has to fix the problem first. I currently work for Breck Inc., as one of the lead graphic designers responsible for assisting in the improvement of audience reach for our clients. This involves social media graphics/animations, front-end web design, and print design.
